About Us.

GOIN was founded in Bengalore in 2010. Today, we’re headquartered in India, with our staff and clients around the globe.

We’re software developers, design thinkers, and security experts. The products and designs we produce are stable, scalable, and long-lasting. Over the past decades, we’ve automated business processes, brought new ideas to market, and built large scale, dynamic infrastructure. These growth-focused, data-intensive businesses benefited from our expertise in scalable infrastructure, project management, and information security.
